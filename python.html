Mandelbrot Set

Figure: matplotlib.pyplot plot of the Mandelbrot set.

I have always had a fascination with the Mandelbrot set and its beauty. The non-divergent set was first conceived by Adrien Douady and named after Benoit Mandelbrot set, a pioneer of fractals. The complex fractal has gained a cult following due to its complexity, beauty and unique structure. There are many examples of animations displaying the self-similarity under magnification, videos exampling the set and even t-shirts.
